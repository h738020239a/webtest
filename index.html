Why sound?

Virtually any device with a microphone or speaker is OxySound-ready, and is configurable to use audible or inaudible near-ultrasonic frequencies.

OxySound is designed for simplicity, eliminating connectivity headaches and simplifying everyday tasks like connecting to Wi-Fi networks, sharing contact details, and making peer-to-peer payments.

It's trusted by companies and developers across the world, from nuclear power stations to children's toys.
